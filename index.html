    Pure Eigens

    TypeScript
    ClientDilks Lab
    Date April 2014

    EyeTrack relies on data provided by Eyelink hardware. Experiments are conducted allowing subjects to view images of scenes, faces, and objects naturally, attempting to identify the patterns between the picture's contents and eye movements. These patterns are then used as training data for PyBrain neural networks. The result is a network that can tell what category of object a subject is looking at by just inputting their eye movements.
